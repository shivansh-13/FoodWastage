Stop Food Wastage!

Food wastage is a global issue that has severe consequences on the environment, economy, and humanity. We urge you to take a moment and think before wasting food. Together, we can make a difference!

Consequences of Food Wastage:

  • Environmental Impact: Wasted food contributes to greenhouse gas emissions, deforestation, and loss of biodiversity.
  • Economic Loss: Billions of rupees are lost each year due to food wastage, impacting farmers, businesses, and consumers.
  • Resource Misuse: Wasting food also means wasting the resources used in producing, processing, and transporting it, such as water, land, and energy.
  • Food Insecurity: While millions of people around the world suffer from hunger, a significant amount of food produced is never consumed.
